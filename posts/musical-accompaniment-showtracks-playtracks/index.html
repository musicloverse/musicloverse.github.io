Best Way to Create Custom Backing Tracks for Keyboard Players

In this article, we will discuss how to create custom backing tracks or accompaniments for keyboard players. Backing tracks are essential for musicians, especially when learning songs or wanting to play along with music. Whether you play with a band or as a solo musician, having great backing tracks can greatly enhance your performance and practice sessions.

Introducing Karaoke Version#

Karaoke Version is a website that specializes in providing custom backing tracks for various instruments, including vocals, guitar, drums, bass, and piano. It offers a wide range of tracks that you can customize according to your needs. Additionally, Karaoke Version allows you to isolate specific tracks so you can focus on particular instruments or parts of a song.

Making Custom Backing Tracks#

To create your own custom backing tracks, visit the Karaoke Version website and search for the track you want to customize. Once you’ve found the track, you can begin working with it. The website offers various options and features to tailor the backing track to your preferences.

For example, if you want to remove the keyboard parts to practice playing along, you can do so with the custom backing track. This allows you to rehearse with a band-like experience without the original keyboard playing alongside you. Similarly, you can isolate and solo specific instruments or tracks, such as the keyboard or organ, to study and learn certain parts more effectively.

Features and Options#

Karaoke Version offers numerous features and options to customize your backing tracks. For example, you can choose to have a backing click track or an intro count for better timing and rhythm during practice sessions. These options are particularly useful for drummers and percussionists who need a reference point during performances or rehearsals.

Additionally, you can adjust the volume and panning of each instrument track, allowing you to create a personalized mix. This way, you can emphasize or mute certain instruments according to your preferences and playing style. The website also provides the ability to change the key of the song, which is beneficial for singers or musicians who need to perform in a different key than the original recording.

Benefits of Custom Backing Tracks#

Using custom backing tracks has several benefits for keyboard players and other musicians. First and foremost, it allows for more flexible practice sessions, especially when playing without a band. With custom backing tracks, you can simulate a band-like experience and improve your overall playing and timing.

Moreover, custom backing tracks are effective teaching tools. You can study songs or specific parts of songs with greater clarity and focus. As demonstrated by renowned musician Rick Piatto, isolating instrument tracks can help in understanding the nuances and techniques used by the original artists. It also enables you to create your own interpretations and arrangements of songs.

Affordable and Accessible#

Creating custom backing tracks with Karaoke Version is both affordable and accessible. The website offers a vast selection of tracks at reasonable prices, usually around $3. This allows you to explore different songs and genres without breaking the bank. Furthermore, once you purchase a track, you can download it and create multiple mixes and versions. This gives you the freedom to experiment and adapt the backing tracks to your specific needs and preferences.

Conclusion#

Custom backing tracks are a valuable resource for keyboard players and musicians in general. They provide a range of benefits, including improved practice sessions, enhanced learning experiences, and more personalized performances. With platforms like Karaoke Version, creating custom backing tracks has become easier and more accessible than ever before. So, if you’re looking to elevate your musical journey, consider incorporating custom backing tracks into your practice routine and performances.
