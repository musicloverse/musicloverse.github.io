How to Create Viking Folk Music: A Beginner's Guide to Simple Viking Instruments

Are you inspired by the epic Viking TV series and the enchanting music that accompanies them? Do you dream of composing your own Viking folk songs? Look no further, as we delve into the world of Viking music and explore the steps to create your very own Viking folk masterpiece. In this article, we will guide you through the process of making Viking folk music, from choosing a band name to selecting the perfect instruments and crafting your unique melody. Get ready to embark on a musical journey that celebrates the spirit of the Vikings!

Choosing a Suitable Band Name#

Every Viking folk band needs a strong and captivating name. To align with the genre’s traditions, opt for a single-word name inspired by ancient Scandinavian languages. Seek inspiration from old Norse literature, such as the Edda, or choose a name that references your favorite character from Viking mythology. However, remember to conduct a thorough Google search to ensure that your chosen name is not already in use by another band.

Crafting a Captivating Logo#

Once you have chosen a band name, it’s essential to create a captivating logo that represents your Viking folk identity. A simple and effective method is to write your band name using ancient runes. Alternatively, you can create a unique logo by combining the runes from your band name to form a bind rune. Your logo should embody the mystical and ancient feel of Viking culture, attracting the attention of potential listeners.

The Basics of Viking Folk Music#

Viking folk music is characterized by its simplicity and timeless sound. One of the key elements of this genre is the use of drones – sustained notes that play continuously throughout a song. These drones create an ancient and folky ambiance that transports listeners to the era of the Vikings. When composing your Viking folk song, focus on harmonies that complement the chosen drone note. Since modulating to different keys is not possible with drones, it is crucial to select simple harmonies.

Choosing the Right Instruments#

Selecting the right instruments is vital in capturing the essence of Viking folk music. Traditional Viking instruments, such as the talharpa and jaw harp, can beautifully accompany your melody. If you don’t have access to these instruments, don’t worry! The internet offers a plethora of free VST plugins that imitate the sounds of various instruments, enabling you to experiment and find the perfect fit for your Viking folk song. Remember, the instrument you choose will significantly influence the “Viking-ness” and “Faulkiness” of your composition.

Crafting the Melody#

The melody is the heart and soul of any song, and it is no different in the realm of Viking folk music. Let your creativity soar as you choose an instrument to carry your melody. Whether it is the ethereal sound of a flute or the powerful resonance of a violin, the choice is yours. If you prefer digital instruments, explore the vast array of free VST plugins available online. While crafting your melody, keep in mind that minor scales evoke melancholy and darkness, ideal for an introspective Viking folk song. Alternatively, major scales offer a more jubilant vibe, perfect for a lively tavern drinking song.

Creating Rhythms and Percussion#

The rhythm section of your Viking folk song provides the foundation and adds depth to your composition. Shaman drums, frame drums, timpanis, and toms are excellent choices for achieving the ritualistic and powerful sound associated with Viking folk music. By layering multiple drum tracks or using drum samples, you can achieve a massive percussive sound that transports listeners to ancient Viking gatherings. Additionally, consider incorporating other percussive elements such as tambourines, shakers, sticks, and stones to add texture and variety to your music.

Crafting Engaging Lyrics#

Just as Viking folklore is rich in stories, your Viking folk song should convey a compelling tale. Seek inspiration from public domain Norse mythology materials available online to craft your lyrics or find inspiration for your own unique narrative. Incorporate fragments from popular Viking tales, such as the Edda, to immerse listeners in the mythical world of the Vikings. Experiment with multiple vocal layers, ranging from choir-like vocals to whispers or even growls, to add a touch of Viking authenticity to your lyrics.

Mastering Your Track#

After composing your Viking folk masterpiece, it’s time to give it that professional touch by mastering your track. Experiment with different audio effects to add depth and richness to your percussion and vocals. Adding generous amounts of reverb to your mix can create a massive sound that transports listeners to vast Viking landscapes. Continuously fine-tune the mix until you achieve the desired balance between instruments and vocals. Once you are satisfied with the final mix, it’s time to export your track and prepare for the next steps.

Presenting Your Viking Folk Identity#

Now that your Viking folk song is complete, it’s time to embrace the spirit of the Vikings and present your music to the world. Before stepping into the limelight, captivate your audience with promotional pictures that embody the Viking aesthetic. Dress in suitable Viking attire, such as tunics, and consider adding war paint to your face for an authentic touch. Venture into the depths of the forest to capture enchanting photographs, incorporating snowy trees against the sky. Enhance your images with your unique band logo, resulting in a captivating album cover that encapsulates the essence of your music.

In Conclusion#

Congratulations! You have embarked on a musical journey through the world of Viking folk music. By following these steps, you can create your very own Viking folk masterpiece that leaves listeners captivated and yearning for more. Remember, Viking folk music celebrates simplicity and ancient traditions, and by infusing your own unique style and creativity into the genre, you can create a truly captivating musical experience. So grab your favorite Viking instrument, channel your inner Viking spirit, and let the melodic tales of the Norsemen come to life through your music. Cheers to a new era of Viking folk music!
